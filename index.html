Components/Parts

The quality of the components and parts on a bike are directly related to the quality of the bike they are on. Therefore, espescially when looking at newer bikes where cheap and expensive frames are harder to tell apart, being able to identify the quality of a bike's parts is important in assessing the value of a bike

Wheels

The easiest way to tell good wheels/rims apart from bed ones is by looking at they're material. A cheap bike will use chrome plated steel, whereas a nice bike will use an alloyed material. The alloyed material is usually duller and softer looking than the chrome plated material. Another important factor is the shape of the rim. An expensive bike will have more of a rounded shape compared to the sharp edged rims put on cheaper bikes.

Mechanisms

As with the wheels, the first thing you should look at is the material. A nice bike will always use an alloy outfit.
